# **4. Data Management**
---
The final step in your onboarding is learning where the data lives and how to access it.<br>

**MEG Data** <br>
- [Filezilla](https://filezilla-project.org/) is the gateway to the MEG data storage (data are uploaded directly after acquisition in the lab).
- Download the program to get access to the data. 
- Click on this button (xxxx) in the upper left corner to create a new server.
- Log in using your OpenVPN credentials and this gateway: sbdl-gateway.ccns.sbg.ac.at
- Data location: /mnt/sinuhe/data_raw/
- Files are sorted after acquisition date (e.g., 241022) and named after subject code (e.g., 19661028wxyz)
- Drag & drop to copy them to your local computer.

**MRI Data**<br>
- [This](https://mridb.ccns.sbg.ac.at) is where the MRI scans and the subject data are stored. You can simply access the website and don't need to download any program/app here. 
- Access it using your user-account (the b1234567 number + the respective password).

**Eye-tracking Data**<br>
- The eye-tracking data lies [here](https://plusacat.sharepoint.com/sites/AG_Braindynamics-Staff/Freigegebene%20Dokumente/Forms/AllItems.aspx?id=%2Fsites%2FAG%5FBraindynamics%2DStaff%2FFreigegebene%20Dokumente%2FStaff%2Fdata%2Feyetracker&viewid=60687c99%2Da2fc%2D463c%2D9b04%2Dd733b343eb55&web=1&CT=1744632882281&OR=OWA%2DNT%2DMail&CID=39263125%2D14d5%2D4c27%2D80ea%2D748735b47b2e&FolderCTID=0x0120005DE86C714A40404A907C5D7BCB165C79)
- You can ask [Fabian Schmidt](https://ccns.plus.ac.at/labs/auditory/members/fabian_schmidt/) to give you access to the folder.
- To understand what the columns mean, read [here](https://www.vpixx.com/manuals/psychtoolbox/html/TRACKPixxDemo1.html).
