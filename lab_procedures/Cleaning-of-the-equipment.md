# MEG : Cleaning of the equipment

* Clean and disinfect every equipment that was in contact with the participant! For more detailed information please see also chapter **MEG-safety**!
* Clean carefully to remove everything but also in a way that the equipment stays intacte.g. don't directly spray on the button box as it will become too wet and it will do more harm than good; instead wipe with the cleaning/disinfection-tissues from the blue bucket next to MSR-door!be carefully with HPI-coils (fragile!)
* Desinfect also the Polhemus stylus pen and the glasses (also MRI-glasses, if used; with a special soft tissue or is the glas scratch-free?) after each preparation
* for auditory experiments: also make sure to remove used 'earplugs'
* In case you have used an EEG-cap:fill the wash-bowl with lukewarm water soak used EEG-cap for ~30 minutesrinse the cap with lukewarm water by using the showerhead**Attention: don't evert the cap - it is very fragile - wires break easily****Dry cap**in the air (best way is to stuff it with a towel)When dry, disinfect cap with alcohol from the spray bottle
